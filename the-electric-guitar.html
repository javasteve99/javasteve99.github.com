the electric guitar

Click here to view the project.

"The Electric Guitar" is a presentation created for a semester-long project on Flash. The purpose of the class was to complete the semester with one cohesive presentation/website that would educate the user on a specific topic. The project was required to have an animated intro, a timeline or history of the topic, an interactive quiz that would test the user on their knowledge, and a section for crediting sources.
