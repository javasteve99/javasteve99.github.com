Steve Hickey is a UX Strategist at Fresh Tilled Soil in Watertown, MA. Educated in print design, he jumped quickly to the web after graduation. A proud and meticulous craftsman, Steve loves a good challenge. He's the company typography nerd and is always keeping up with the latest trends and techniques. He's a craft beer aficionado and brewer, an aspiring handyman, and an unapologetic Star Wars fan. He is also the Director of AUX, Fresh Tilled Soil's UX apprenticeship program.
