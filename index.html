I’m a UX strategist and a product design educator, with a no-nonsense approach to building things that matter.

Originally trained in print design, I became fascinated with the web during my final year in the visual design program at UMass Dartmouth. Now I work with Fresh Tilled Soil in Watertown, MA where I lead teams that build products for companies that put their users first. I’m the Director of AUX, our user experience apprenticeship program, as well as a writer and conference speaker, and former podcast co-host.

During the day I’m a meticulous craftsman, the company typography nerd, and a relentless seeker of good challenges. After hours I’m a craft beer aficionado and brewer, an aspiring handyman, and an unapologetic Star Wars fan.
