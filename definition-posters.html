definition posters

For this project I chose two words and then designed a poster around each of them. The goal was to use the definitions as part of the poster and to make them work as a series. The final result was a comment on my belief that neither political party is particularly good at figuring out what Americans need. Instead they spend their time whining (ululate) and complaining (stridulate). This is the first poster.

And this is the second poster. The have the same composition, but in reverse of each other.

Here are the posters together. The center of the poster turns into a war of words as the two ideologies clash.
